# --description--

`Real-time` means happening instantly, without any delay. For example, when you chat with someone online, you are communicating in `real-time`.

`Collaboration` means working together with others to achieve a common goal. For example, when you and your classmates work together on a group project, you are `collaborating`.

`Real-time project collaboration` means working together on a project with team members instantly, even if they are in different locations. This often involves using software or apps that allow instant communication and updates.
