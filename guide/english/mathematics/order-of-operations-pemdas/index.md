Order of operations is the rules for the order in which you should undertake an expression if two or more operations are in it.

There are 3 common acronyms that are associated with the oreder of operations; these are BODMAS, BIDMAS and PEMDAS. BODMAS means brackets, orders, multiplication, addition and subtraction. BIDMAS means brackets, induces, division, multiplication, addition and subtraction. PEMDAS means  parenthesis, exponents, multiplication, division, addition and subtraction. BIDMAS and BODMAS are commonly used in the UK as acronyms for the order of operations, while PEMDAS is more commonly used in the united states.
