Generating prime numbers and checking them on paper is not the most exiting activity you can think of, so these are my suggestions 
for a quick prime number generator:

Online generator

Browserling,
It's a really handy tool and is very easy to use
[online PrimeGenerator] - https://www.browserling.com/tools/prime-numbers

Asecurity,
Another great website that provides a simple prime number generator with your desired specifications
[online PrimeGenerator] - https://asecuritysite.com/encryption/random3?val=8


Coding challenge
Prime number generator could be a great coding exercise, but you might want to look up some of the methods you could use
like BruteForce or Sieve Of Eratosthenes:
https://en.wikibooks.org/wiki/Some_Basic_and_Inefficient_Prime_Number_Generating_Algorithms
