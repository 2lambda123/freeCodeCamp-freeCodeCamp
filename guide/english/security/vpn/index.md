## VPNs
A <bold>VPN</bold> is a <I>Virtual Private Network</I>; it is a series of servers located in different physical locations, and through which internet traffic may be directed to obfuscate the user's physical location. VPNs are used to bypass some country restrictions on internet content, or to obscure the identity of someone working outside the bounds of legal regulations.
