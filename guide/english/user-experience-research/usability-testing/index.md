## Usability Testing

Usability testing is a user-centered interaction design technique implemented to evalute a product by testing it on actual users in order to determine how well people can use it, and if they can use it for its intended purpose. Examples of common usability testing are often applied to food, consumer products, devices, as well as websites and apps. Usability testing involves systematic observation under controlled conditions, and qualitative and usability testing can be comingled to better understand the users' motivations and wants.
