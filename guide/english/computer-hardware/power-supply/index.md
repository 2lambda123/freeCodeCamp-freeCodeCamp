## Power Supply

A Power Supply Unit (PSU) supplies power to a computer. It converts AC power into steady low-voltage DC power for the internal components. 

A PSU is usually not user serviceable. For your safety, it is wise to never open a power supply unit.

##Troubleshooting 
  
  1.In some of the LOBs, There is test called PSU Bist using this test, you will understand exact problem.
  2.Also on power supply there is small LED if it blinking with amber color then there is issue with PSU.
  3.Swap directly PSU with another KG PSU unit and you can troubleshoot the problem and accordingly get solution.
