## Super Globals

Superglobals are variables built in to PHP that are available across all scopes in a script. This means they can be accessed inside of functions or methods.

$GLOBALS
$_COOKIE
$_ENV
$_FILES
$_GET
$_POST
$_REQUEST
$_SERVER
$_SESSION
